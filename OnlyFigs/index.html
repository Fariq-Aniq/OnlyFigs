We are OnlyFigs

OnlyFigs is for people who think regular fruit is boring. Founded in 2025 in the mysterious lands of Kuala Nerang, Kedah, we're obsessed with figs. Like, maybe too much. We grow 'em, we squish 'em, and we put 'em in jars for you.

Our mission is to put a fig in every hand (with consent, of course) while pretending we know what we're doing. So far, so good!
